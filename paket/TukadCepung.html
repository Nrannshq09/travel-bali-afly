Overview

Discover one of Bali's most unique and photogenic waterfalls, Tukad Cepung. Unlike typical waterfalls, Tukad Cepung is hidden within a cave, where sunlight streams through the opening above, creating magical light beams that illuminate the falling water.

Located in the Bangli regency, this hidden gem requires a short and easy trek through beautiful bamboo forests and small streams. The journey itself is an adventure, and the destination is truly rewarding – a perfect spot for nature lovers and photography enthusiasts.

Tips for Your Visit

  • Best time to visit is between 9 AM - 11 AM when sunlight creates beautiful light beams
  • Wear water shoes or sandals as you'll need to walk through shallow streams
  • Bring a waterproof bag for your electronics
  • Don't forget your swimwear if you want to take a dip
  • Bring a towel and change of clothes
