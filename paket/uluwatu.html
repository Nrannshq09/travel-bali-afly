Luxury Beach & Cliff Temple Experience

Overview

Experience the perfect blend of luxury and spirituality on this full-day tour to Nusa Dua and Uluwatu. Begin your journey at the pristine beaches of Nusa Dua, known for their calm waters and world-class resorts. Then, witness the breathtaking Uluwatu Temple perched on a steep cliff overlooking the Indian Ocean.

As the sun begins to set, be mesmerized by the captivating Kecak Fire Dance performance, telling the story of the Ramayana with a backdrop of the setting sun. This tour offers the perfect introduction to Bali's rich cultural heritage and natural beauty.

Tour Highlights

Nusa Dua Beach

Relax at the pristine white sand beach with crystal clear calm waters, perfect for swimming and water sports.

Uluwatu Temple

Visit the iconic sea temple perched on a 70-meter cliff with panoramic ocean views.

Kecak Fire Dance

Watch the mesmerizing traditional dance performance with sunset as the backdrop.

Photo Spots

Capture stunning photos at the most instagrammable locations in South Bali.
