Tirta Gangga Water Palace & Lempuyang Temple

Overview

Visit the iconic Heaven Gate and the beautiful water palace of Tirta Gangga. Experience the magic of East Bali with stunning mountain views, ancient water palaces, and the famous gate that frames Mount Agung perfectly. This journey takes you through the rich cultural heritage of Bali's eastern region.

Whether you're traveling with family, friends, or as a couple, our East Bali tour package is designed to bring wonder, beautiful photo opportunities, and unforgettable memories against the backdrop of Bali's most iconic landmarks.

Places to Visit

Heaven Gate (Lempuyang)

The iconic gate that frames Mount Agung perfectly, one of Bali's most photographed and sacred temples.

Tirta Gangga

Beautiful water palace with stunning pools, fountains, and koi fish, a former royal palace of Karangasem.

Mount Agung View

Spectacular views of Bali's highest and most sacred volcano from various viewpoints.

Virgin Beach

Optional stop at one of East Bali's pristine white sand beaches with calm blue waters.
