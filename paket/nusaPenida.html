Overview

Discover the raw natural beauty of Nusa Penida, a hidden gem located southeast of Bali. This full-day tour takes you to the island's most iconic landmarks, where you'll be greeted by turquoise waters, white sandy beaches, and dramatic limestone cliffs that will take your breath away.

Choose between our West Trip to visit the famous Kelingking Beach (the "T-Rex"), Broken Beach, Angel Billabong, and Crystal Bay, or our East Trip to explore the stunning Diamond Beach, the iconic Tree House, and Atuh Beach. Both options include round-trip fast boat transfers and a delicious Indonesian lunch.
