Cultural Experience & Sunset Temple

Overview

Discover the cultural heart of Bali on this full-day tour to Ubud and Tanah Lot. Experience the island's rich traditions, stunning landscapes, and spiritual heritage as you visit iconic landmarks and immerse yourself in local culture.

From the playful monkeys at the Sacred Monkey Forest to the breathtaking rice terraces of Tegallalang, and ending with a magical sunset at the legendary Tanah Lot Temple, this tour offers the perfect introduction to Bali's diverse beauty.

Tour Destinations

Monkey Forest

Sacred forest sanctuary home to hundreds of playful long-tailed macaques and ancient temples.

Tegallalang Rice Terrace

Iconic UNESCO World Heritage site with stunning terraced rice paddies and traditional irrigation system.

Bali Swing

Experience the thrill of swinging over the jungle valley with breathtaking views and photo opportunities.

Tegenungan Waterfall

Beautiful waterfall surrounded by lush greenery, perfect for swimming and photo sessions.

Tanah Lot Temple

Iconic sea temple perched on a rock formation, offering spectacular sunset views and spiritual atmosphere.
